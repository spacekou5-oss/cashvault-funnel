G0: Establish Operational Baseline

Leverage: 36/40 | Timeline: Weeks 0-2 (Pre-PMV Sprint) | Time Cost: ~8 hrs one-time
COO Purpose

You are performing a pre-flight inspection on every system before the first piece of content ever drives traffic. This is the highest-leverage work you will ever do, because a broken tool discovered after 10,000 views is a catastrophic leak. A broken tool discovered before the first view is a 5-minute fix. The cost of skipping this phase is not linear. It is exponential. Every tool, every link, every popup, every account connection gets stress-tested NOW, when the stakes are zero and the cost of repair is lowest.

The Senior Content Creator cannot start driving traffic until you confirm the runway is clear. This is the gate. Nothing passes through until you sign off.

COO Actions
  • Full Ecosystem Stress Test (Every Tool, Every Screen, Every Button)
    Open EACH of the 7 tools on mobile (iPhone Safari AND Android Chrome, or use browser DevTools mobile emulation). This is non-negotiable. 90%+ of the audience will experience these tools on a phone screen.

    For each tool, test:
    Autopilot Architecture Map (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/autopilot-system.html)
    Cost of Waiting Calculator (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/cost-of-waiting.html)
    800-Club Checklist (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/800-club.html)
    Freedom Calculator (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/freedom-calculator.html)
    Prompt Vault (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/prompt-vault.html)
    Investment Simplifier (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/Investment-Simplifier.html)
    Budget Calculator (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/Kou's-Budget-Calculator.html)

    Audit checklist per tool: Does it load under 3 seconds on mobile? Do all inputs accept data correctly? Does the result display without visual glitches? Does the Beehiiv email popup fire AFTER the result (not before, not during)? Does the popup button say something specific ("Send My Full Report") not generic ("Submit")? Does the fake loading screen fire before the result (Idleness Aversion)? Does the progress bar start at 15% (Goal Gradient)? Are all external links functional?

    Document every failure in a priority-ranked fix list: Critical (blocks user), Major (degrades experience), Minor (cosmetic). Fix all Critical and Major before declaring Phase 0 complete.
  • Wire the Capture Skeleton (Accounts, Not Sequences)
    This is NOT about building the full email welcome sequence or ManyChat flows (that belongs to G2 in Phase 1). This is about verifying the ACCOUNTS and CONNECTIONS work before you build on top of them.

    Beehiiv: Create account. Verify custom domain (if using one) or confirm default sending domain. Send yourself a test email. Confirm it lands in inbox, not spam. Set up your first subscriber tag ("test-tag"). Manually add a test subscriber and confirm the tag applies. If this breaks, you cannot build the welcome sequence later.

    ManyChat: Create account. Connect to Instagram. VERIFY Instagram is set to Business or Creator mode (ManyChat will not function on a Personal account). Set up ONE test keyword ("TEST"). DM the keyword from a separate account. Confirm ManyChat detects it and sends the auto-reply. If this fails, every keyword flow in Phase 1 is dead on arrival.

    Beehiiv-to-Tool Connection: Verify that the email capture popup on at least ONE tool successfully pushes the subscriber into Beehiiv. Open the tool, trigger the popup, enter a test email, confirm it appears in your Beehiiv subscriber list with the correct source tag. This proves the pipe is connected end-to-end.
  • Competitive Intelligence Infrastructure
    Identify 8-12 accounts in the Gen Z finance creator space. Not to copy them. To map the terrain.

    For each competitor, document: What they charge (free, low-ticket, high-ticket). What format they deliver in (PDF, course, app, template). What pain they claim to solve. What their CTA structure looks like (bio link > landing page > what?). What keywords they use in captions. What their engagement rate feels like (lots of comments vs. ghost town).

    The deliverable is a 1-page gap analysis: "Here is what every competitor does. Here is what NOBODY does. That gap is our attack vector." This feeds directly into G1's MVO concepts in Phase 1. Without this intelligence, the MVO concepts are guesses. With it, they are informed bets.

    Store this in a Google Sheet or Notion page. It becomes a living document updated quarterly.
  • Content Recording Environment Audit
    The Cockpit is the studio. Verify it is production-ready before the Content Creator records anything for public distribution.

    Technical check: Phone camera quality (1080p minimum). Audio clarity (no wind noise, no echo, voice is louder than background). Lighting (face visible, not backlit by the windshield). Camera angle (eye-level or slightly above, stable mount).

    Record 2-3 calibration videos. These are NOT for publishing. They exist to answer one question: "Does this look and sound like a peer or a guru?" Review them with brutal honesty. Adjust the setup until the output matches the brand voice: raw, peer-to-peer, systems-obsessed, not polished and corporate.

    Benchmark the workflow: How long does it take to prep (script outline), record (raw footage), and do a basic edit (trim + captions)? This time cost feeds into the weekly operating checklist design in G4.
  • Audience Seeding Framework
    Before content goes live, the account needs a baseline presence. Zero followers with great content still gets zero distribution (algorithm cold start problem).

    Action: Identify 50 accounts in the target niche (finance creators, Gen Z money accounts, adjacent lifestyle creators). Follow them. Leave 2-3 genuine comments per day on their content (NOT spam, NOT "great post!" but actual value-add commentary). DM 3-5 creators with a genuine compliment or question (relationship building, not pitching).

    Goal: By the time the first piece of content publishes, the account has 30-80 organic followers who already recognize the name. The first video lands with a warm audience, not a void.

    Track: Follower count at start of Phase 0. Follower count at end. Engagement rate on the seeding comments (are people responding?). This is the kindling. Content is the match.

G2: Build the Email Capture Machine

Leverage: 32/40 | Timeline: Week 1 | Time Cost: ~5 hrs one-time
COO Purpose

You are building the plumbing that turns anonymous traffic into identified subscribers and turns identified subscribers into warm leads. The Senior Content Creator drives people to the tools. Your job is to ensure that every person who touches a tool is captured, sequenced, and nurtured automatically. Zero manual intervention after this system is live.

COO Actions
  • Architect the 3-Email Welcome Sequence in Beehiiv
    Set up the automation flow: trigger on new subscriber > Email 1 (immediate) > Email 2 (Day 2 delay) > Email 3 (Day 5 delay). Configure segmentation tags based on entry point (which tool/keyword brought them in). This segmentation becomes critical later for targeted offers.

    You write the structural framework and CTAs. The Senior Content Creator writes the voice, stories, and personality. You own the bones. They own the skin.

    Technical specs: Email 1 subject line must include the specific result/tool name (personalization via merge tag if possible). Email 3 CTA must be a single button (not multiple links). All emails must render correctly on mobile (test in Beehiiv preview + send test to a phone).
  • Configure ManyChat Keyword Automation
    Build 5 keyword trigger flows:

    AUTOPILOT > auto-reply with Autopilot Architecture Map link (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/autopilot-system.html) + email capture prompt
    LEAK > Cost of Waiting Calculator (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/cost-of-waiting.html) + email capture prompt
    ELITE800 > 800-Club Checklist (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/800-club.html) + email capture prompt
    MYNUMBER > Freedom Calculator (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/freedom-calculator.html) + email capture prompt
    CHEAT > Prompt Vault (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/prompt-vault.html) + email capture prompt

    Each flow structure: keyword detected > send tool link message > 30 second delay > send "Want the full breakdown? Drop your email at [tool URL]" follow-up. Two messages, not one. The delay creates anticipation (Idleness Aversion).

    Verify: Instagram account is set to Business/Creator mode (ManyChat requires this). Test every keyword from a separate account.
  • Verify Email Capture Popups on All Web Apps
    Audit every tool in the ecosystem. Each must have a working Beehiiv email capture popup that triggers AFTER the user receives their result.

    Tools requiring verification: Freedom Calculator, Cost of Waiting Calculator, 800-Club Checklist, Autopilot Architecture Map, Investment Simplifier (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/Investment-Simplifier.html), Budget Calculator (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/Kou's-Budget-Calculator.html).

    Popup timing rules (Psychological Warfare Suite): fire AFTER result delivery (not before). Progress bar in popup starts at 15% (Goal Gradient). Button text must be specific: "Send My Full Report" not "Submit" (Uncertainty Anxiety reduction). Include a fake loading screen before showing the result: "Calculating inflation adjustment..." (Idleness Aversion).
  • End-to-End Funnel Testing
    Walk every path on mobile (this is where 90%+ of the audience will experience it):

    Path 1: See IG Reel > click bio link > land on tool > use tool > get result > popup fires > enter email > receive Email 1 immediately > receive Email 2 on Day 2 > receive Email 3 on Day 5.

    Path 2: See IG Reel > DM keyword > ManyChat auto-reply > click tool link > use tool > popup > email captured > welcome sequence fires.

    Document every friction point: slow load times, broken links, popup not firing, emails landing in spam, formatting issues on mobile. Fix all before the Content Creator starts driving traffic.

G4: Automate the Maintenance Layer

Leverage: 30/40 | Timeline: Weeks 2-4 | Time Cost: ~5 hrs one-time + 15 min/week
COO Purpose

At 5-8 hours per week, every repeatable task that touches the founder's hands is theft. Your job is to systematize everything that doesn't require human creativity so the founder's time goes exclusively to content creation, audience connection, and strategic decisions. You build the operating rhythm. You maintain the machine.

COO Actions
  • Set Up Content Scheduling Infrastructure
    Select and configure a scheduling tool (Later, Buffer, or native IG scheduler). Create the account, connect Instagram and YouTube. Build the template workflow: Content Creator batch-records videos > uploads to scheduling tool > COO schedules across 2 weeks (Monday/Thursday cadence). This eliminates the daily "open app and post" friction. The Content Creator records. You distribute.
  • Build the Weekly Operating Checklist
    Create a single-page operational checklist that defines exactly what happens each day. This is not for you. This is for the FOUNDER to execute without making decisions. Your job is to design the rhythm, not run it.

    Monday: Post Video 1 (auto-scheduled). Engage 15 min.
    Tuesday: Engage 15 min. DM 5 target accounts.
    Wednesday: Send weekly email. Engage 15 min.
    Thursday: Post Video 2 (auto-scheduled). Engage 15 min.
    Friday: Engage 15 min. DM 5 target accounts.
    Saturday: Batch record 2-4 videos (biweekly). Or: PMF testing / DM conversations.
    Sunday: CEO Scorecard audit (15 min). Schedule next 2 weeks of posts. Review metrics.

    Deliver this as a printable doc. The founder tapes it to the wall.
  • Configure Metrics Tracking
    Set up a simple weekly dashboard (can be a Google Sheet or Notion page) tracking:

    New email subscribers this week | Total email list size | Email open rate | Email click rate | IG follower count | Avg views per video | Engagement rate (interactions/views) | ManyChat keyword triggers fired | Waitlist signups (during PMF testing)

    Update every Sunday during the CEO audit ritual. This data feeds the Scorecard. Without it, the audit is guesswork.
  • Establish CEO Audit Infrastructure
    The CEO Self-Audit Scorecard app is already built. Your job is to embed it into the weekly rhythm. Every Sunday evening: founder opens the Scorecard, rates all 12 duties, locks in the audit, identifies the lowest score, and that duty becomes the focus for the next week.

    Set a recurring calendar reminder. Make it non-negotiable. The Scorecard is the accountability loop that prevents drift.
