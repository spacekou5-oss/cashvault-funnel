G2: Build the Email Capture Machine

Leverage: 32/40 | Timeline: Week 1 | Time Cost: ~5 hrs one-time
COO Purpose

You are building the plumbing that turns anonymous traffic into identified subscribers and turns identified subscribers into warm leads. The Senior Content Creator drives people to the tools. Your job is to ensure that every person who touches a tool is captured, sequenced, and nurtured automatically. Zero manual intervention after this system is live.

COO Actions
  • Architect the 3-Email Welcome Sequence in Beehiiv
    Set up the automation flow: trigger on new subscriber > Email 1 (immediate) > Email 2 (Day 2 delay) > Email 3 (Day 5 delay). Configure segmentation tags based on entry point (which tool/keyword brought them in). This segmentation becomes critical later for targeted offers.

    You write the structural framework and CTAs. The Senior Content Creator writes the voice, stories, and personality. You own the bones. They own the skin.

    Technical specs: Email 1 subject line must include the specific result/tool name (personalization via merge tag if possible). Email 3 CTA must be a single button (not multiple links). All emails must render correctly on mobile (test in Beehiiv preview + send test to a phone).
  • Configure ManyChat Keyword Automation
    Build 5 keyword trigger flows:

    AUTOPILOT > auto-reply with Autopilot Architecture Map link (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/autopilot-system.html) + email capture prompt
    LEAK > Cost of Waiting Calculator (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/cost-of-waiting.html) + email capture prompt
    ELITE800 > 800-Club Checklist (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/800-club.html) + email capture prompt
    MYNUMBER > Freedom Calculator (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/freedom-calculator.html) + email capture prompt
    CHEAT > Prompt Vault (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/prompt-vault.html) + email capture prompt

    Each flow structure: keyword detected > send tool link message > 30 second delay > send "Want the full breakdown? Drop your email at [tool URL]" follow-up. Two messages, not one. The delay creates anticipation (Idleness Aversion).

    Verify: Instagram account is set to Business/Creator mode (ManyChat requires this). Test every keyword from a separate account.
  • Verify Email Capture Popups on All Web Apps
    Audit every tool in the ecosystem. Each must have a working Beehiiv email capture popup that triggers AFTER the user receives their result.

    Tools requiring verification: Freedom Calculator, Cost of Waiting Calculator, 800-Club Checklist, Autopilot Architecture Map, Investment Simplifier (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/Investment-Simplifier.html), Budget Calculator (https://spacekou5-oss.github.io/cashvault-funnel/Kou's-Budget-Calculator.html).

    Popup timing rules (Psychological Warfare Suite): fire AFTER result delivery (not before). Progress bar in popup starts at 15% (Goal Gradient). Button text must be specific: "Send My Full Report" not "Submit" (Uncertainty Anxiety reduction). Include a fake loading screen before showing the result: "Calculating inflation adjustment..." (Idleness Aversion).
  • End-to-End Funnel Testing
    Walk every path on mobile (this is where 90%+ of the audience will experience it):

    Path 1: See IG Reel > click bio link > land on tool > use tool > get result > popup fires > enter email > receive Email 1 immediately > receive Email 2 on Day 2 > receive Email 3 on Day 5.

    Path 2: See IG Reel > DM keyword > ManyChat auto-reply > click tool link > use tool > popup > email captured > welcome sequence fires.

    Document every friction point: slow load times, broken links, popup not firing, emails landing in spam, formatting issues on mobile. Fix all before the Content Creator starts driving traffic.

Success

All 5 ManyChat flows responding correctly. Welcome sequence firing on every new subscriber. Email 1 open rate above 50%. All 6 tool popups verified working on mobile. Zero manual DM responses needed for keyword triggers.

Failure

ManyChat not triggering (check IG account type). Emails landing in spam (check Beehiiv domain settings). Popups not firing (check JavaScript errors in browser console). Open rate below 30% (subject lines need rework).

Handoff to Senior Content Creator

The Content Creator needs to know WHICH keywords to say in videos and WHAT the user will experience after clicking.

G4: Automate the Maintenance Layer

Leverage: 30/40 | Timeline: Weeks 2-4 | Time Cost: ~5 hrs one-time + 15 min/week
COO Purpose

At 5-8 hours per week, every repeatable task that touches the founder's hands is theft. Your job is to systematize everything that doesn't require human creativity so the founder's time goes exclusively to content creation, audience connection, and strategic decisions. You build the operating rhythm. You maintain the machine.

COO Actions
  • Set Up Content Scheduling Infrastructure
    Select and configure a scheduling tool (Later, Buffer, or native IG scheduler). Create the account, connect Instagram and YouTube. Build the template workflow: Content Creator batch-records videos > uploads to scheduling tool > COO schedules across 2 weeks (Monday/Thursday cadence). This eliminates the daily "open app and post" friction. The Content Creator records. You distribute.
  • Build the Weekly Operating Checklist
    Create a single-page operational checklist that defines exactly what happens each day. This is not for you. This is for the FOUNDER to execute without making decisions. Your job is to design the rhythm, not run it.

    Monday: Post Video 1 (auto-scheduled). Engage 15 min.
    Tuesday: Engage 15 min. DM 5 target accounts.
    Wednesday: Send weekly email. Engage 15 min.
    Thursday: Post Video 2 (auto-scheduled). Engage 15 min.
    Friday: Engage 15 min. DM 5 target accounts.
    Saturday: Batch record 2-4 videos (biweekly). Or: PMF testing / DM conversations.
    Sunday: CEO Scorecard audit (15 min). Schedule next 2 weeks of posts. Review metrics.

    Deliver this as a printable doc. The founder tapes it to the wall.
  • Configure Metrics Tracking
    Set up a simple weekly dashboard (can be a Google Sheet or Notion page) tracking:

    New email subscribers this week | Total email list size | Email open rate | Email click rate | IG follower count | Avg views per video | Engagement rate (interactions/views) | ManyChat keyword triggers fired | Waitlist signups (during PMF testing)

    Update every Sunday during the CEO audit ritual. This data feeds the Scorecard. Without it, the audit is guesswork.
  • Establish CEO Audit Infrastructure
    The CEO Self-Audit Scorecard app is already built. Your job is to embed it into the weekly rhythm. Every Sunday evening: founder opens the Scorecard, rates all 12 duties, locks in the audit, identifies the lowest score, and that duty becomes the focus for the next week.

    Set a recurring calendar reminder. Make it non-negotiable. The Scorecard is the accountability loop that prevents drift.

Success

Zero time spent on manual distribution after Week 4. Weekly checklist followed 4+ consecutive weeks. CEO audit completed every Sunday. Metrics dashboard updated weekly. Founder spends less than 10 min/week on admin tasks.

Failure

Founder still posting manually after Week 4. CEO audits skipped (accountability loop broken). No metrics being tracked (flying blind). Checklist abandoned after Week 2 (design was too complex, simplify it).
