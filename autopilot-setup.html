The real problem

Your willpower isn't broken. Your paycheck routing is.

Right now, your money lands in one account. Everything comes out of that same account: rent, food, gas, subscriptions, impulse buys, and whatever is "left over" is supposed to become savings.

Nothing is ever left over. And you blame yourself every single time.

Manual saving has a 92% failure rate within 90 days. Not because people are irresponsible. Because the system is designed to fail. When savings requires a daily decision, the decision eventually goes the other way. The fix isn't more discipline. It's removing the decision entirely.

An Autopilot system moves your savings, emergency buffer, and investment money on payday, automatically, before you see the balance. You set it up once. It runs every paycheck. The money you used to spend is now routed before you can touch it. Not because you're better with money. Because the machine is.
