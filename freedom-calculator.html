How is this calculated?

The Rule of 25

Your Freedom Number = Monthly Income x 12 months x 25. This is the total amount you need invested so that a 4% annual withdrawal covers your expenses indefinitely. It is the most widely used benchmark in early retirement planning.

The 8% Return

We use 8% average annual return based on the S&P 500 historical performance since 1957. This is pre-tax and nominal. Actual returns vary year to year. Some years negative, some 20%+. Over 20+ year horizons, the average converges toward this range.

What This Does NOT Include

This calculator does not deduct taxes, investment fees, or inflation from projections. It also does not select the investments to get there. For that, the Investment Simplifier handles it after you enter your email. Think of this as the destination. The Simplifier shows you the map.
